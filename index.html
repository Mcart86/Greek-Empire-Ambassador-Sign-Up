About the Program

Greek Empire is a branded merchandise company built specifically for college and university life. As a Campus Ambassador, you'll help connect people at your school with Greek Empire while gaining experience in sales, marketing, networking, and entrepreneurship. You'll build relationships on campus, help organizations create custom merchandise, and earn commission on orders you bring in.

*Being a member of a Fraternity or Sorority is not a requirement to be a Campus Ambassador.

OVERVIEW

You are the target market. You understand what's trending, what resonates, and what makes people stop scrolling. This program rewards you for putting that knowledge to work — creating authentic, engaging content for Greek Empire's social media channels across TikTok and Instagram.

HOW IT WORKS
  • Create content for Greek Empire for approval to be posted and the opportunity to be featured on our TikTok and Instagram, or share it on your own social media after it is approved
  • Earn cash rewards based on engagement
  • All metrics must be verified by Greek Empire management before payouts are issued

RULES & VERIFICATION
  • All content must be submitted to Greek Empire's Empire Social Review Portal
  • Required monthly ambassador content qualifies for the SIP Program
  • Metrics are verified by management before any payout
  • Screenshots of analytics must be submitted for verification within 7 days of milestone hit
  • Metrics are measured within a 14-day window from the post date
  • Coordinated sharing within friend groups to inflate numbers is not permitted — organic reach only
  • Use of bots, purchased followers, or paid engagement of any kind is strictly prohibited and will result in disqualification
  • Rewards stack — hitting views and shares on the same video earns both rewards
  • Reward amounts are subject to change based on company budget and marketing plans
  • Management's decisions on award criteria and disputes are final

All ambassadors are required to use the Greek Empire CRM. No exceptions. This is how your activity is tracked, commissions are calculated, and the brand learns what's working on the ground.

WHAT TO LOG

Every interaction with a campus organization must be entered into the CRM. This includes:

  • Every organization contact you speak with — name, email, phone number, and any additional notes
  • Order status from first conversation through to placement
  • Wins, objections, and feedback from organizations
  • Campus observations — what's resonating, what the competition is doing, what's coming up

You are Greek Empire's eyes and ears on campus. What you observe directly shapes how the brand grows.

CAMPUS INSIGHTS

Beyond orders, your most valuable contribution is real-time intelligence from the ground. Report what you're seeing and hearing:

  • Which organizations are ordering and why
  • What competitors are doing or offering
  • What's resonating with students — and what isn't
  • Customer satisfaction or dissatisfaction after orders
  • Upcoming events, bid days, and organization milestones to target

You'll receive a bi-weekly automated check-in to capture this. Take it seriously — this feedback shapes the brand.
